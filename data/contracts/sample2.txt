EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into on January 15, 2024, between TechCorp Inc., 
a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and John Smith ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee shall serve as Software Developer and perform duties as assigned by the Company.

2. COMPENSATION
Company shall pay Employee a salary of $75,000 per year, payable bi-weekly.

3. BENEFITS
Employee shall be entitled to health insurance, 15 days paid vacation, and 10 sick days annually.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice period.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary company information.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of Delaware.
